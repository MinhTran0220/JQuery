Rule to Play the Game

Try to get any random number start of the game.


There are four crystals below. Please clicking on a crystal, and you will add points to your total scores.


You will win the game by matching your total score to random number, if you lose the game, your total points will goes over the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden, until you click on it.


Each time when you start play, the game will change different points of each crystal
